# Diagnostic Criteria Reference

## Cardiovascular

### Acute Coronary Syndrome (ACS)
- **Troponin elevation** > 99th percentile
- **Chest pain** characteristics: crushing, pressure, radiating to arm/jaw
- **ECG changes**: ST elevation/depression, T wave inversion

### Heart Failure (ACC/AHA)
- **Stage A**: Risk factors only
- **Stage B**: Structural changes, no symptoms
- **Stage C**: Structural changes with symptoms
- **Stage D**: Refractory heart failure

### Atrial Fibrillation (CHA2DS2-VASc)
- Congestive heart failure: 1 point
- Hypertension: 1 point
- Age >=75: 2 points
- Diabetes: 1 point
- Stroke/TIA: 2 points
- Vascular disease: 1 point
- Age 65-74: 1 point
- Sex category (female): 1 point

## Pulmonary

### COPD (GOLD Criteria)
- FEV1/FVC < 0.70
- Mild: FEV1 >= 80%
- Moderate: 50% <= FEV1 < 80%
- Severe: 30% <= FEV1 < 50%
- Very Severe: FEV1 < 30%

### Pulmonary Embolism (Wells Score)
- Clinical signs of DVT: 3.0
- PE likely or diagnosis: 3.0
- Heart rate >100: 1.5
- Immobilization/surgery: 1.5
- Previous DVT/PE: 1.5
- Hemoptysis: 1.0
- Malignancy: 1.0

## Metabolic

### Diabetes Diagnosis (ADA)
- FBG >= 126 mg/dL
- 2h OGTT >= 200 mg/dL
- HbA1c >= 6.5%
- Random BG >= 200 mg/dL with symptoms

### Metabolic Syndrome (NCEP ATP III)
3+ of 5:
- Waist: >102 cm (men), >88 cm (women)
- Triglycerides: >=150 mg/dL
- HDL: <40 mg/dL (men), <50 mg/dL (women)
- BP: >=130/85 mmHg
- Fasting glucose: >=100 mg/dL

## Neurology

### Stroke (NIHSS)
- 0-4: Minor stroke
- 5-15: Moderate stroke
- 16-20: Moderate to severe
- 21-42: Severe stroke

### Meningitis (Lumbar Puncture)
| Parameter | Bacterial | Viral | TB |
|-----------|-----------|-------|-----|
| Opening Pressure | Elevated | Normal | Elevated |
| WBC | >1000 PMN | 10-500 Lymph | 100-500 Lymph |
| Glucose | <40 mg/dL | Normal | <45 mg/dL |
| Protein | >150 mg/dL | <100 mg/dL | >100 mg/dL |
| Gram Stain | Positive | Negative | Negative |

## Red Flags

### General
- Chest pain with diaphoresis
- Sudden severe headache
- Focal neurological deficits
- Respiratory distress
- Altered mental status
- Unexplained weight loss >10%

### Pediatric
- Fever >48h in infant <3 months
- Petechial rash with fever
- Bulging fontanelle
- Paradoxical breathing

# Web Research Sources

## Authoritative Sources

### Academic/Government
- PubMed - Medical research
- arXiv - Preprints
- Government websites (.gov)
- University websites (.edu)

### News/Media
- Major newspapers (NYT, Guardian, etc.)
- Wire services (Reuters, AP)
- Established blogs

### Industry
- Industry association websites
- Company annual reports
- Trade publications

## Source Evaluation

| Criterion | Good | Poor |
|-----------|------|------|
| Author | Named expert | Anonymous |
| Date | Recent | Outdated |
| Citations | Links to sources | No references |
| Purpose | Inform | Sell/advertise |
| Review | Peer-reviewed | No review |

## Search Tips
- Use quotes for exact phrases
- Use site: for specific websites
- Use - to exclude terms
- filetype: for specific documents
